ofxNeurons

This is a fork from @pabloriera's Spiking Neural Networks after Izhikevich's model:

Izhikevich, E. Simple model of spiking neurons. IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks 14, 6 (2003), 1569â€“ 1572.

The code works as an @openframeworks application. Future work is to make it and ofx-addon.

The project is momentarily at rest but @pabloriera will eventually reactivate it.

TOTAL number of neurons is hardcoded in testApp.h.

'r' key resets and seeds random values.

OSC events are sent under the /neuron label to localhost:12345

The mouseX controls the currents.
